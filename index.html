About us

Dunes is a solution company focused on making fresh accessible for people through innovative methods. We ensure this by implementing the best of methods in the world of purification and supply technology. For us, is a gift more than it is a need. And this gift should be enjoyed with the best of experience.

Dunes' is derived from the nature of our home country, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Blessed with one of the largest sand deserts on earth, dunes are a core element of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Proud to be providing an essential service to the citizens & residents of Saudi, it was befitting for us to derive the name from the very essence of the country.
